Open-source Hackathon @Algebra 2024

Published On: November 19, 2024

Join us for Croatia’s one of the first open-source hackathons, organized by Algebra University in partnership with DENT, a project by the Linux Foundation! This unique event will take place at the Algebra Spark Event Space in Zagreb on Saturday, December 14, 2024, from 8:00 to 20:00. With a prize fund of 3,000 EUR, the hackathon is designed for students and professionals eager to tackle challenges in network configuration and management. Seize the opportunity to collaborate with top experts, explore solutions for remote management, configuration resilience, and telemetry integration.

About DENT Project

As a Linux Foundation project, DENT utilizes the Linux Kernel, Switchdev, and other Linux-based projects as the foundation for building a new, standardized network operating system without abstractions or overhead. All underlying infrastructure — including ASIC and Silicon for networking and datapath — is treated equally, simplifying existing abstractions, APIs, drivers, low-level overhead, and other open-source software. DENT brings together silicon vendors, ODMs, SIs, OEMs, and end users from all sectors to enable the transition to disaggregated networks.

About the Event

One of the first open-source hackathons in Croatia, organized by Algebra University in collaboration with DENT, a Linux Foundation project. Participants, in teams of up to three members, will work on challenges such as cloud system management, remote packet capture, and telemetry improvement. This is a perfect chance to showcase your skills, innovate, and compete for valuable prizes.

Who is the Hackathon for?

The hackathon is open to students and IT professionals eager for challenges in networking technologies and collaboration with leading mentors. Participants will work on topics such as remote management, configuration resilience, and telemetry integration, aiming for innovation and the creation of solutions that push the boundaries of network systems. Whether you’re passionate about cloud-managed systems, remote packet capture, or telemetry enhancement, this is your chance to collaborate, innovate, and showcase your skills. Don’t miss the opportunity to delve into advanced network solutions and compete for significant prizes.

What to Expect

Valuable Prizes: The prize fund includes 2,000 EUR for first place and 1,000 EUR for second place. Mentors: Zlatan Morić, Head of the Cybersecurity program; Vedran Dakić, lecturer at Algebra University; and Bruno Banelli, lecturer at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing in Zagreb and Algebra University, will provide mentorship and support. Networking Opportunities: Connect with peers and industry experts. Hands-on Experience: Develop and test your solutions with mentor support.

Key Information

  • Date and Time: Saturday, December 14, 2024, from 8:00 to 20:00
  • Location: Algebra Spark Event Space, Algebra University, Zagreb
  • Partners: Linux Foundation, DENT Project
  • Prize Fund: 3,000 EUR
  • Sponsors: Red Bull
  • Capacity: Up to 30 participants

Don’t miss the opportunity to participate in the first open-source hackathon in Croatia, shaping the future of networked systems.
